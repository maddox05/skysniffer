## About SkySniffer

SkySniffer is an educational iOS app that uses advanced AI technology to help you learn about contrail formation, persistence patterns, and atmospheric conditions through analysis of your sky photos.

### What is SkySniffer?

SkySniffer analyzes photos of the sky to identify and explain contrail patterns, atmospheric conditions, and persistence levels. Using GPT-4 Vision AI, the app provides detailed educational analysis to help you understand atmospheric phenomena and aviation weather patterns.

### Common Questions

**Q: What photos work best?**
A: Clear photos of the sky showing contrails or cloud formations. The app works best with photos that clearly show aerial vapor trails.

**Q: Why does the app say "Not a Sky Image"?**
A: The AI detected that your photo doesn't show the sky or contrails. Try taking a photo that includes the sky and visible contrails.

**Q: How accurate are the results?**
A: SkySniffer uses advanced AI for educational analysis. Results are AI-generated and should not be considered definitive scientific conclusions. See our disclaimer below.

**Q: Why do I need camera/photo permissions?**
A: Camera permission allows you to photograph the sky directly. Photo library permission lets you select existing photos for analysis.

**Q: Is my data shared?**
A: No. All photos are stored locally on your device. Only the selected image is sent to our AI service for analysis. See our [Privacy Policy](https://maddox05.github.io/skysniffer/privacy.html) for details.

**Q: How can I delete my scan history?**
A: Swipe left on any scan in your history and tap "Delete". All scans are stored locally and can be deleted anytime.
